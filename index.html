WHY FAITH UNIVERSITY?

TUITION-FREE

Faith University College doesn't charge for college courses, course materials, or annual enrollment. You only pay our minimal fees as you go, never upfront.

FLEXIBILITY

Faith University College is 100% on-campus college for now. But you can control your schedule by studying some days a week or by picking a credit hours.

QUALITY

Faith University College is an accredited Liberian University. Our academic leadership includes scholars from top universities around the world and in Liberia.

SUPPORT

Our classes are very large and every student has a Program Advisor from their first day of classes all the way until graduation.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Diploma>Certificates>Degrees->Associate>B.sc>BBA

100% Approved And Accredited By The National Commission On Higher Education.
